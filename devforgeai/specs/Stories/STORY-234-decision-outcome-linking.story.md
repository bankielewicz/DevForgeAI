# Story: Link Decisions to Story Outcomes (Optional)

## Description

**As a** Framework Analyst,
**I want** to correlate planning decisions with story outcomes,
**so that** I can measure decision effectiveness.

**Note:** This story is marked optional and may be deferred based on priority.

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC#1: Decision-Outcome Linking

**Given** plan decisions,
**When** linking to story outcomes,
**Then** decision links to story status (delivered, deferred, failed).

---

### AC#2: Effectiveness Measurement

**Given** linked decisions,
**When** analyzing outcomes,
**Then** decision effectiveness score is calculated.

---

### AC#3: Success Pattern Identification

**Given** effectiveness scores,
**When** analyzing patterns,
**Then** decision patterns correlated with success are identified.

---

## Definition of Done

### Implementation
- [ ] Decision-outcome linking
- [ ] Effectiveness scoring
- [ ] Pattern identification

### Quality
- [ ] All 3 acceptance criteria verified
- [ ] Statistical significance >0.7
